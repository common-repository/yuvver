== Description ==

Yuvver allows your clients to share your Website, Products or Affiliate Program with their friends by E-mail

== Installation ==

1. Upload 'Yuvver directory' to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Verify your domain
4. Set an API key
5. Create new invitation in Yuvver section
6. Put the shortcode of the invitation in any post or page editor

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What can i do with Yuvver? =
With Yuvver you can create many types of email invitations and let your clients share your Website, Products or Affiliate Program with their friends by their E-mail.

= How to use in Yuvver? =
Each invitation have a unique shortcode.
You can position it in your post or page editor.
Yuvver render it to a button that allows your clients to share your Website, Products or Affiliate Program with their friends by E-mail.

= How Yuvver is better from others social share buttons? =
With Yuvver your clients can send a personal invitation to your Website, Products or Affiliate Program with their friends by their E-mail.
